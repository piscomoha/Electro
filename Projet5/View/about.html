OUR STORY

Born from a vision to democratize futuristic technology, ELECTROSTORE was founded by a team of ex-NASA engineers and Silicon Valley visionaries. We witnessed firsthand how cutting-edge innovations were locked behind corporate walls, inaccessible to everyday consumers.

In 2025, we decided to change that. Starting from a small garage in San Francisco, we've grown into the world's premier destination for next-generation technology, serving over 2 million customers across 45 countries.
